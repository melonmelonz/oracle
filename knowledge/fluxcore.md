# Fluxcore

## What it is

Fluxcore is a VPP arbitrage project — a virtual power plant concept built as an
interview deliverable. A virtual power plant aggregates many small, distributed
energy resources (home batteries, EVs, flexible loads) and operates them as if
they were one dispatchable plant; arbitrage is the act of buying and storing
energy when it is cheap and discharging when it is expensive.
